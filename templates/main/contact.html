Frequently Asked Questions

What services does the Jordan Consulate in Nepal provide?
The Jordan Consulate in Nepal provides various services including visa applications, passport renewals, document authentication, and consular assistance to Jordanian citizens in Nepal.
How can I apply for a visa to Jordan?
To apply for a visa to Jordan, you need to submit an application form, valid passport, passport-sized photographs, and other required documents. Please contact the consulate for specific requirements based on your visa type.
What are the working hours of the consulate?
The Jordan Consulate in Nepal is open from Monday to Friday, 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM. The consulate is closed on Jordanian and Nepali public holidays.
