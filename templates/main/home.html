About Us

Welcome to the Honorary Consulate of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan in Kathmandu, Nepal

The Honorary Consulate of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan in Kathmandu, Nepal serves as a consular service provider in Kathmandu. Our mission is to strengthen diplomatic relations, facilitate travel and provide support for Jordanian citizens in Nepal. We are committed to fostering cultural exchange, enhancing trade opportunities and promoting mutual understanding between the two nations.
