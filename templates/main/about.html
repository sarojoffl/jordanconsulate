About the Office

Your Gateway to the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan in Nepal

Our Mission

The Office of the Honorary Consul of The Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan in Nepal serves as the primary diplomatic representation of Jordan in Nepal. We are dedicated to strengthening bilateral relations and providing comprehensive consular services.

Core Objectives:

  • Facilitate diplomatic relations between Jordan and Nepal
  • Provide efficient consular services
  • Promote cultural understanding
  • Support business and trade initiatives

Our Services

  • Document authentication and legalization
  • Consular assistance for Jordanian citizens
  • Trade and investment facilitation
  • Cultural exchange program coordination
  • Educational cooperation support

Contact Information
